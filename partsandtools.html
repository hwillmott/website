Things You Will Need

Frame

most of this I got at a hardware store but am including an amazon link to a similar item

  • Paper roll: https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002UJFAO0
  • Tape: https://www.amazon.com/Scotch-Magic-Tape-3105-Inches/dp/B0009F3P3U
  • Project board (got this at an art supply store)
    • White, thin, but sturdy cardboard
    • Needs to be at least 116 cm x 39 cm
  • Wood (2cm x 1cm x ?)
    • Need 2 pieces 115.5 cm long and 2 pieces 31.6 cm long
    • Don't cut until the end! You will need to use this wood as a guide for drawing straight lines
    • Make sure you get decent quality wood (I got 'project' wood) so you can use it to draw straight lines
  • Brackets (and screws): https://www.amazon.com/Stanley-Hardware-75-6111-Corner-Braces/dp/B00004Z0R1
  • Craft wire (for hanging on wall - need 2 extra screws for this): https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B003LTGS4E
  • Nails (for hanging on wall): https://www.amazon.com/National-Hardware-V7710-Nails-Galvanized/dp/B005MJFA8G
  • 20 (long) twist ties or 40 short ones with the paper stripped off, or some other moldable, thin wire
