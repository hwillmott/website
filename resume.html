ABOUT

Harriet is from Victoria, BC, Canada. She graduated with a Bachelors of Computer Science in 2014 with Honours and Distinction. Her Honours project focused on grouping similar artists by applying graph clustering algorithms to musical album metadata. During her undergrad, she completed internships at some of the local software companies in Victoria, doing primarily web and enterprise application development. After graduating, she started full time as a developer on the XAML Experiences team on Visual Studio at Microsoft. After a year and a half on XAML Experiences, she switched to working on the Cortana Proactive Experiences team. Harriet's interests outside of work include hiking, skiing, cycling, yoga, cooking, reading, drawing, and making LED art installations and programming visualizations for them in Processing.
