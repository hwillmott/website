This website is a chronicle of my artistic adventures, starting in late 2015. I began with Processing animations, then started doing mixed media animations by drawing the backgrounds on paper. Then I spent some time programming LEDs with Processing, and made my LED panel, a jacket, a ceiling installation, and a wall installation. Most recently, I took a drawing course (motivated by the mixed-media animations) and have since been fascinated by drawing people.
