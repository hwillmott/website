Now the hard part: attach the LEDs to the jacket.

I decided to sew little sleeves for each individual LED strip by hand because I wanted the fabric to be stretched around the strip evenly. I experimented with making sleeves with a sewing machine, but it was too hard to feed the strips through unless is the sleeve was so wide that it was too loose anyways. I also thought about making one big sleeve, but there needs to be some way to keep the strips lined up with each other. You really do not want them moving around or overlapping each other.

Then I sewed the strips together to make a panel. I did this with the LEDs plugged in and on to make sure the pixels were lined up nicely.

Then I sewed the panel onto the sleeve of my jacket just by stitching the edges to the sleeve.
